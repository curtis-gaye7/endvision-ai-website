AI Processing

EndVision AI uses artificial intelligence to help break down goals into actionable steps, recommend habits, and analyze productivity patterns. Our AI systems process your goal descriptions, habit data, and time tracking information to provide these services. This processing is essential to the core functionality of the application.

The AI features may analyze:
